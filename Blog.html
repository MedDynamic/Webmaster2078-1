Take Care of your Teeth!

April 7, 2021
Although brushing your teeth twice everyday may seem like a tedious task, it is essential for having good oral health. Brushing your teeth twice a day can kill up to 99.9% of all germs in your teeth; it is really astonishing. Along with the germs being killed, all of the food and plaque in your teeth are removed from brushing, and who really wants food stuck in their teeth all day and night? Also, make sure to floss your teeth and use mouth wash every now and then to make sure your teeth are shining bright!
